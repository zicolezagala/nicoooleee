📸 STREET PHOTOGRAPHY
▼

Tired San Mateo Municipal College students walk slowly under the intense heat, wearing matching uniforms. A simple yet relatable moment of resilience and dedication.

1. What story does your photo tell?
Tired SMMC students walking under the heat, wearing matching academic uniforms—a relatable moment of student life, not dramatic but real.

2. Why did you choose this subject?
Because I’ve experienced the same thing many times. It’s personal and familiar.

3. What elements make your photo effective?
The harsh noon lighting makes you feel the heat. The candid composition—woman walking toward camera, group behind—feels natural. The timing captures a quiet, unposed moment. The emotion is subtle: tired endurance with a touch of humor.

4. What challenges did you encounter?
It was hard because it wasn’t scripted. I worried people might get angry if they noticed me taking their picture without permission.

5. How can you improve next time?
Use a better quality camera for sharper details. Get closer or zoom in for clearer expressions. Shoot in burst mode to catch the most natural frame.
